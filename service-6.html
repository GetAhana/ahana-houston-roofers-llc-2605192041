When to call us

These five situations call for a professional roof inspection to document condition and catch problems early.

FAQ — Roof inspections

How long does a roof inspection take?

A roof inspection typically takes 30 to 60 minutes depending on home size and roof accessibility. We provide a written report with photos same day or next business day, explaining condition and any recommended repairs.

Should I get a roof inspection before selling my home?

Yes—home buyers increasingly ask for roof inspections as part of due diligence. A pre-listing inspection shows buyers the roof's condition and gives sellers a chance to address issues proactively, which can increase buyer confidence.

How often should I have my roof inspected?

Annual inspections catch small issues like loose flashing, missing shingles, or ventilation gaps before they become leaks. In Texas, post-storm inspections also catch hail or wind damage before the next heavy rain.
