Roof Leak Repair in Houston

Roof leaks often trace to failed flashing, damaged shingles, or ventilation problems. We find the source and make lasting fixes—with photos for your insurance claim when needed.

When to call us

Roof leaks sometimes show clear signs. Watch for these five common indicators of potential water intrusion.

  • Water stains on ceilings or walls inside the home indicate active or recent water penetration from the roof.
  • Soft spots, sagging, or dark patches on shingles suggest rotting wood underneath that needs immediate attention.
  • Flashing separation from walls or chimneys allows water to flow behind and into the structure.
  • Missing or damaged shingles expose the underlayment and wood to rain and wind-driven moisture.
  • Granule buildup in gutters or visible bald spots on shingles signal shingle degradation and weakened protection.

FAQ — Roof repair

What do I do if my roof is leaking?

Call (713) 555-0198 immediately and describe the leak location and water damage you see. If it's after hours or weekend, leave a message—we treat emergency calls as priority and return calls quickly. We can often tarp a leak same day to prevent further damage.

How much does roof leak repair cost?

Leak repair costs depend on the source. A simple shingle fix may cost a few hundred dollars; flashing work or structural damage costs more. We provide written estimates after inspection so you know the exact scope before work starts. No surprises.

What happens after you repair the leak?

We trace the leak to its source—often flashing, valleys, vents, or poor ventilation—make the repair, and send before-and-after photos. If insurance is involved, we provide documentation to support your claim. Photos become part of your home record.
