Common
questions

How long has Houston Roofers been in business?
We've been serving Houston since 2008, growing from a family trade roofing background into a full-service team. Today we handle everything from emergency leak repairs to complete replacement systems with manufacturer backing.
How fast can you respond to a roof leak or storm damage?
We prioritize same-week visits for standard calls and treat storm damage as urgent. Weekend calls roll to Monday morning unless a slot opens. You'll get a realistic timeline when you call or text.
Do you charge for estimates?
Every job gets a written estimate before work begins. We walk through options, explain manufacturer warranties, and never charge for the inspection visit—whether you move forward or not.

Ready to learn more about our roofing process?

We offer free written estimates and same-week scheduling for most Houston-area calls. Contact us to discuss your roof.
