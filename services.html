Services

Services for Houston Homeowners

Houston Roofers offers six core roofing and gutter services for residential and light commercial properties across Houston and nearby areas. Whether you're facing storm damage, planning a full replacement, or need routine inspection, we deliver free written estimates and transparent pricing.

POPULAR

Flagship roofing services

Roof replacement, repair, and storm damage work are our flagship lines. Each gets the same care: honest estimates, same-week scheduling when possible, and photo documentation so you own your project from start to finish.

Roof replacement

Full tear-offs and re-roofs with manufacturer-backed systems, attic ventilation checks, and magnet sweep before we leave. We handle everything from asphalt shingles to metal standing seam, with written estimates and two-year labor warranty on every installation in Houston and nearby areas.

Learn more

Roof repair

Leak tracing, shingle and flashing fixes, and emergency tarping when storms hit—photos for insurance when you need them. We locate the source, make lasting repairs, and document every step so you have clear records for your adjuster or next owner.

Learn more

Storm damage

Hail and wind inspections, documentation for adjusters, and repair-or-replace guidance without pressure. We assess damage scope, explain coverage options, and help you make informed decisions about the best path forward for your Houston home.

Learn more

Commercial roofing

Low-slope repairs, protective coatings, and maintenance plans for retail centers and light industrial buildings. We understand commercial roof lifecycle and budget cycles.

Learn more

Gutters

Seamless aluminum gutters, custom downspout routing, and leaf guards engineered for Texas downpours. Gutters protect your foundation and prevent water damage to siding and landscaping while completing your home's appearance.

Learn more

Roof inspections

Pre-listing, post-storm, and annual roof inspections with written photo reports. Know your roof's condition before issues become emergencies—peace of mind in one visit.

Learn more
EXTENDED SERVICES

Additional roofing and home protection services

Beyond our six core services, Houston Roofers offers specialized work to keep your home protected year-round. Attic ventilation, siding repair, emergency stabilization, and maintenance contracts round out our full-service roofing and home protection capabilities.

Attic ventilation

Ridge vents and intake balance for hot-climate attics. Proper ventilation extends roof life, reduces attic heat buildup in summer, and prevents moisture condensation in winter.

Request scope

Siding repair

Hardie board and vinyl siding patch work after storm hits. When wind or hail damages siding panels, we match materials and complete repairs to protect your home's weather seal.

Request scope

Emergency board-up

Temporary protective tarping and board-up after major wind or tree damage. Emergency stabilization prevents further water intrusion and damage while you coordinate permanent repairs.

Maintenance plans

Annual tune-ups and minor sealant touch-ups to keep your roof and systems in peak condition. Preventive maintenance catches small issues before they become expensive emergencies.
