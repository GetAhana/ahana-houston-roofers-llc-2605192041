Terms of Use

Houston Roofers operates under Texas licensing, insurance, and ethical service standards. We respect your property and your budget.

Website use

This website provides information about Houston Roofers services and is for general reference. Estimates are based on site visits; photos and descriptions are representative of our work but not guarantees of identical results. Pricing is subject to on-site inspection and may vary based on accessibility, material availability, and actual damage scope.

Estimates & scheduling

Written estimates are provided free of charge. Estimates include labor, materials, warranty terms, and timeline. Once approved, we schedule work within one week when possible. Payment terms are discussed before work begins—typically 50% deposit at start, final payment upon completion and inspection approval.

Limitation of liability

All work is covered by our two-year workmanship warranty on labor and manufacturer material warranties when products are registered per label. Homeowner insurance may cover storm damage; we provide photo documentation to support claims. Warranty claims are handled by calling (713) 555-0198 with your invoice.
